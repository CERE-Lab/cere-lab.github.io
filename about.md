## Who we are
**Computing Education Research at Emeris (CERE)** is a research lab focused on improving teaching, learning, and assessment in computing through rigorous, practice-relevant research.

<div class="callout">
<strong>Core idea:</strong> Computing education is a specialized field focused on teaching computer science, programming, and computational thinking.
</div>

## Our mission
To advance computing education using ICT-enabled, evidence-based approaches that are scalable in real classroom settings.

## Our values
- **Evidence first:** we evaluate impact, not just adoption
- **Student-centred:** learning experiences designed for inclusion and success
- **Ethical and responsible:** privacy, fairness, and academic integrity matter
- **Collaboration:** educators, students, and partners co-create solutions

## Research approach
Our work typically includes:
- Research design and ethics
- Instrument design (rubrics, surveys, logs, observation)
- Analysis (quant + qual)
- Practical outputs (guides, templates, exemplars, prototypes)

## Partnerships
We welcome collaboration with:
- School of Computer Science (Emeris)
- Faculties and teaching & learning units
- Schools, NGOs, and industry partners
- Postgraduate researchers and research assistants
