Investigating Generative AI Integration in Teaching & Learning

Exploring responsible, ethical, and transformative uses of generative AI to enhance computational thinking, collaboration, and academic integrity in higher education.

Research Focus Areas

AI as Cognitive Scaffold

Using generative AI to support drafting, coding, problem-solving, and conceptual clarification.

Collaborative AI Learning

Integrating AI tools within social coding spaces and Communities of Practice.

Assessment Innovation

Redesigning assessment strategies to incorporate AI transparency and reflective practice.

Ethics & Governance

Developing institutional frameworks for responsible AI integration in education.

Strengthened problem-solving and algorithmic reasoning skills.
