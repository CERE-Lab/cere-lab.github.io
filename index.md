## Welcome to CERE

**Computing Education Research at Emeris (CERE)** is a research lab dedicated to advancing teaching, learning, and assessment in computing.

### Focus Areas

- Computing Pedagogy
- Learning Analytics
- Robotics & AI
- EdTech Innovation
- Curriculum & Graduate Readiness

### What We Do

- Conduct applied computing education research
- Publish academic and practitioner outputs
- Partner with academics and institutions
- Support postgraduate students' research
