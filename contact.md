## Collaborate with CERE
We welcome collaboration with educators, schools, EdTech partners, and researchers who want to improve computing education through evidence-based innovation.

### What to include in an enquiry
- Who you are and your role
- What problem you want to solve (or investigate)
- Context (module/level, cohort size, constraints)
- What data is available (if any) and what ethics considerations apply
- Timeline and expected outputs
